<p><strong>The Surface Protection Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adhesive Free</li><li>Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface protection films are used to shield surfaces from damage during manufacturing, transportation, or installation. They are categorized into **adhesive-free** and **adhesive-based** types. **Adhesive-free films** rely on static cling or mechanical bonding, making them easy to remove without residue, ideal for sensitive surfaces like glass or electronics. **Adhesive-based films** use a light or strong adhesive for better adhesion, suitable for rough or uneven surfaces like metals or plastics. Both types cater to diverse industries, balancing protection and ease of removal.</p></p>
