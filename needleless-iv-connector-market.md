<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Needleless IV Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Fluid Displacement</li><li>Negative Fluid Displacement</li><li>Neutral Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needleless IV connector market is categorized into three main types based on fluid displacement mechanisms: </p><p>1. **Positive Fluid Displacement**: These connectors ensure that fluid is pushed into the catheter during use, minimizing blood reflux and maintaining patency.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Negative Fluid Displacement**: This type operates by drawing fluid back into the connector, which can lead to blood reflux but allows for easier medication administration.</p><p>3. **Neutral Displacement**: These connectors create a balance, preventing both blood reflux and backflow, offering a safer option for medication delivery while preserving catheter function.</p></p>
