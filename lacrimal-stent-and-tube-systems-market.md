<p><p>The lacrimal stent and tube systems market includes monocanalicular and bicanalicular stents, both essential for treating lacrimal drainage issues. Monocanalicular stents are designed for a single canaliculus, facilitating easier insertion and removal, while bicanalicular stents cater to both canaliculi, providing more comprehensive drainage support. These systems are crucial in managing conditions like epiphora and canalicular obstructions, enhancing patient comfort and improving outcomes during surgical procedures. Their different designs cater to varying clinical needs and patient conditions.</p></p>
