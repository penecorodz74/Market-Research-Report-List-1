<p><ul><li>Oxidation Base (Primary Intermediate) Dyes</li><li>Coupler (Secondary Intermediate) Dyes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oxidation dyes market consists of two primary categories: oxidation base (primary intermediate) dyes and coupler (secondary intermediate) dyes. Oxidation base dyes serve as the foundational colorants, undergoing chemical changes during the dyeing process to develop vibrant hues. Coupler dyes interact with oxidation bases to enhance or modify colors, providing depth and stability. Together, these dyes are essential in various applications, including textiles and hair coloring, allowing for a wide range of shades and lasting results.</p></p>
