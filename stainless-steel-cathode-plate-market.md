<p><ul><li>Ladle Copper Cathode Plate</li><li>Copper Clad Steel Cathode Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel cathode plate market is categorized into two main types: ladle copper cathode plates and copper clad steel cathode plates. Ladle copper cathode plates are primarily used in electrorefining processes for copper production, offering excellent conductivity and durability. In contrast, copper clad steel cathode plates feature a layer of copper bonded to steel, enhancing strength and reducing costs while maintaining conductivity. Both types are essential in the mining and metallurgy sectors for efficient metal recovery.</p></p>
