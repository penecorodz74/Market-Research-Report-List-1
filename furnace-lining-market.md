<p><strong>The Furnace Lining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acidic Type</li><li>Basic Type</li><li>Neutral Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The furnace lining market is categorized into three main types: acidic, basic, and neutral. Acidic linings, made from materials like silica, resist acidic slags, making them ideal for industries processing non-ferrous metals. Basic linings, often composed of materials like magnesite, are designed for handling basic slags, suitable for steel and iron production. Neutral linings, typically formed from products like alumina, can withstand both acidic and basic environments, providing versatility in various thermal applications. Each type is tailored to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
