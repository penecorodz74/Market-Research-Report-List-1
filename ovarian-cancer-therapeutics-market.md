<p><p>The ovarian cancer therapeutics market encompasses several innovative treatment types, including PARP inhibitors, PD-L1 inhibitors, and angiogenesis inhibitors. PARP inhibitors target cancer cells with defective DNA repair mechanisms, enhancing treatment efficacy. PD-L1 inhibitors boost the immune response against tumors by blocking proteins that prevent immune recognition. Angiogenesis inhibitors disrupt the formation of new blood vessels, effectively starving tumors of necessary nutrients. These therapeutics represent advanced approaches to improve outcomes for ovarian cancer patients, reflecting ongoing research and development in oncology.</p></p>
