<p><strong>The cDNA Library Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phage Library</li><li>Plasmid Library</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cDNA library market can be categorized into two primary types: phage libraries and plasmid libraries. A phage library utilizes bacteriophages to clone cDNA inserts, offering high efficiency and ease of screening, making it ideal for large-scale gene expression studies. In contrast, plasmid libraries use plasmids as vectors for cDNA insertion, providing stable expression and easy manipulation in host cells. Both types serve essential roles in genomics and molecular biology, enabling researchers to explore gene functions and interactions effectively.</p></p>
