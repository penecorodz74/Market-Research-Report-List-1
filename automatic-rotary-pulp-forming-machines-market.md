<p><p>Automatic rotary pulp forming machines are designed for producing molded pulp products efficiently. The market is divided into two main types based on the number of sides they operate on: six-side and eight-side machines. Six-side machines typically form products using a combination of vacuum and pressure across six surfaces, allowing for versatile shapes. Eight-side machines offer enhanced precision and consistency by utilizing additional forming surfaces, resulting in higher-quality molded items suitable for various applications, including packaging and foodservice.</p></p>
