<p><p>The optical pellet sorter market includes two primary types: chute type and belt type sorters. Chute type sorters utilize gravity-driven channels to separate pellets based on color and size, making them efficient for high-throughput applications. Belt type sorters, on the other hand, employ a conveyor belt system to control the flow of pellets, allowing for precise sorting and gentle handling. Both types enhance product quality and processing efficiency in various industries, including plastics and agriculture.</p></p>
