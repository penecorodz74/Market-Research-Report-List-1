<p><p>The paint booths market is primarily categorized into open type and closed type paint booths. Open type paint booths allow for airflow and natural ventilation, making them suitable for small-scale operations and less toxic materials. In contrast, closed type paint booths provide a controlled environment with enhanced filtration and exhaust systems, ensuring safety and compliance with environmental regulations. These booths are ideal for industrial applications, where precision and cleanliness are crucial for high-quality finishes. Both types cater to specific needs within the painting industry.</p></p>
