<p><ul><li>Receiver</li><li>Transceiver</li><li>Transmitter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft ADS-B Receiver market encompasses three primary types: receivers, transceivers, and transmitters. Receivers gather data from aircraft broadcasts, providing situational awareness and enhancing safety. Transceivers combine both transmitting and receiving functionalities, allowing for two-way communication of position information between aircraft and ground stations. Transmitters, while less common, broadcast an aircraft's position and status to others. Together, these components improve air traffic management and contribute to more efficient and secure flight operations in the aviation industry.</p></p>
