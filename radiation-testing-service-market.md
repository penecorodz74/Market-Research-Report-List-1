<p><strong>The Radiation Testing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Dose Radiation Sensitivity (ELDRS) Testing</li><li>High Dose Rate (HDR) / Total Ionizing Dose (TID) Gamma Irradiation Testing</li><li>Single Event Effects (SEE) Testing</li><li>Neutron Irradiation Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radiation Testing Service market encompasses various testing methodologies to assess the resilience of electronic components and systems to different types of radiation. Low Dose Radiation Sensitivity (ELDRS) Testing evaluates the performance of devices under low doses of radiation over time. High Dose Rate (HDR) and Total Ionizing Dose (TID) Gamma Irradiation Testing measure the impact of high radiation doses on components. Single Event Effects (SEE) Testing assesses transient faults from individual radiation particles, while Neutron Irradiation Testing focuses on effects caused by neutron radiation exposure. Each testing type targets specific vulnerabilities in devices for applications in aerospace, defense, and medical sectors.</p></p>
