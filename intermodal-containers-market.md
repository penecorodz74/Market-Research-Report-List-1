<p><p>Intermodal containers encompass various types tailored for specific cargo needs. Dry freight containers are designed for standard cargo like textiles and electronics. Flat rack containers support large, bulky items without roofs. Insulated containers provide thermal protection for sensitive goods, while refrigerated containers maintain low temperatures for perishables. Open top containers facilitate the transport of taller or unusual cargo that requires crane loading. Each type optimizes logistics efficiency and protects the integrity of diverse cargo during intermodal transport.</p></p>
