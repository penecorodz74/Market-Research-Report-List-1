<p><p>The semiconductor equipment pumps market is primarily categorized into two types: liquid pumps and vacuum pumps. Liquid pumps are designed to transport fluids used in semiconductor fabrication processes, ensuring precise delivery and control of chemicals, cooling fluids, and other liquids essential for manufacturing. Vacuum pumps, on the other hand, create and maintain low-pressure environments, crucial for processes like deposition and etching, where contamination control and material consistency are vital for producing high-quality semiconductor devices. Both types play essential roles in the semiconductor manufacturing ecosystem.</p></p>
