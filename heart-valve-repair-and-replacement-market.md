<p><p>The heart valve repair and replacement market primarily includes Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement (TAVR) and Transcatheter Mitral Valve Repair (TMVR). TAVR is a minimally invasive procedure used to replace narrowed aortic valves, improving blood flow and heart function. TMVR focuses on repairing mitral valves that are leaking or not functioning properly, enhancing mitral valve competency. Both approaches utilize advanced catheter-based techniques, reducing recovery times and surgical risks, while catering to patients ineligible for traditional open-heart surgery.</p></p>
