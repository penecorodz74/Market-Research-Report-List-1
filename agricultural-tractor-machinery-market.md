<p><p>The agricultural tractor machinery market is primarily divided into two types: wheel tractors and crawler tractors. Wheel tractors are designed for high-speed transportation on roads and stable terrains, making them suitable for various agricultural tasks like plowing and hauling. In contrast, crawler tractors are equipped with tracks instead of wheels, providing better traction on uneven, muddy, or soft soils, making them ideal for tasks in challenging terrains. Both types cater to different farming needs, enhancing productivity and efficiency.</p></p>
