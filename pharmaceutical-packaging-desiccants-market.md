<p><p>The pharmaceutical packaging desiccants market comprises three main types: silica gel, molecular sieves, and montmorillonite clay. Silica gel is widely used for moisture absorption due to its high efficiency and safety. Molecular sieves offer precise moisture control with their uniform pore sizes, making them suitable for sensitive pharmaceuticals. Montmorillonite clay, a natural absorbent, provides good moisture retention and is an environmentally friendly option. Each type serves to maintain the stability and efficacy of pharmaceutical products by controlling humidity levels.</p></p>
