<p><p>Float level sensors are devices used to detect the level of liquids in tanks and containers. The market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Magnetic Float Level Sensors** utilize a magnetic float that rises and falls with the liquid level, activating switches or generating signals without direct contact with the liquid. </p><p>2. **Mechanical Float Level Sensors** rely on a buoyant float coupled to a mechanical arm or rod, which physically moves in response to liquid levels. </p><p>3. **Others** include variations like capacitive or ultrasonic sensors, expanding application versatility across industries.</p></p>
