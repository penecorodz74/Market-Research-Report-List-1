<p><ul><li>Deposition Technology:CVD</li><li>Deposition Technology:MBE</li><li>Deposition Technology:HVPE</li><li>Deposition Technology:MOVPE</li><li>Deposition Technology:LPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The II-V compound semiconductor market is segmented based on deposition technologies used for material growth. Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) employs gases to create thin films, while Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) uses molecular beams for precise layer construction. Hydride Vapor Phase Epitaxy (HVPE) is effective for growing high-quality crystals, while Metal-Organic Vapor Phase Epitaxy (MOVPE) utilizes metal-organic precursors for efficient film deposition. Liquid Phase Epitaxy (LPE) involves crystallization from a liquid solution, offering an alternative approach for development.</p></p>
