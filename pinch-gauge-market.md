<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Pinch Gauge</li><li>Mechanical Pinch Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pinch Gauge market is primarily divided into two types: hydraulic and mechanical pinch gauges. Hydraulic pinch gauges utilize fluid pressure to measure force accurately, making them suitable for high-precision applications. In contrast, mechanical pinch gauges operate through a spring mechanism, providing a simpler and more portable option for general use. Both types are essential in various industries, including manufacturing and healthcare, for assessing grip strength and applying force in diverse tasks, catering to different precision and application needs.</p></p>
