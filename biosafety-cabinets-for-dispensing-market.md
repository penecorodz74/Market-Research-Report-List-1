<p><strong>The Biosafety Cabinets for Dispensing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class II A Type</li><li>Class II B Type</li><li>Class III Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biosafety cabinets (BSCs) are essential laboratory equipment designed to provide a sterile workspace when handling biological materials. Class II A Type cabinets recirculate air within the cabinet while filtering it, offering personnel protection but minimal protection for the environment. Class II B Type cabinets also recirculate air but exhaust it externally, providing higher environmental protection. Class III Type cabinets are completely sealed and gas-tight, offering maximum protection for both personnel and the environment, ideal for highly infectious agents.</p></p>
