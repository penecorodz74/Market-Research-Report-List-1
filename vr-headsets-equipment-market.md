<p><p>The VR headsets and equipment market comprises two primary types: PC VR headsets and all-in-one (AIO) VR headsets. In contrast, all-in-one VR headsets integrate hardware and software in a standalone device, providing portability and user-friendly experiences without the need for external devices, making them popular for casual users and wireless experiences.</p></p>
