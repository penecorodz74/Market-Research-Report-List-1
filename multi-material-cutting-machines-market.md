<p><p>The multi-material cutting machines market comprises several types, each utilizing distinct technologies for cutting various materials. Laser cutting machines use focused laser beams for precision cutting, ideal for thin materials. Plasma cutting machines employ high-thermal plasma jets for effective cutting of thick metals. Flame cutting machines utilize a combination of oxygen and fuel gas to cut through metal, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Knife cutting machines use sharp blades for clean cuts in softer materials, making them ideal for textiles and packaging.</p></p>
