<p><p>Compacted Graphite Iron (CGI) is a versatile cast iron alloy characterized by its unique microstructure, where graphite appears in a compacted form, enhancing strength and stability. The market for CGI can be segmented into two main types: spheroids and nodules. Spheroidal CGI features graphite in rounded shapes, providing improved ductility and fatigue resistance, whereas nodular CGI exhibits graphite in oval or elongated forms, offering superior tensile strength and thermal conductivity, making it suitable for various industrial applications, including automotive and heavy machinery.</p></p>
