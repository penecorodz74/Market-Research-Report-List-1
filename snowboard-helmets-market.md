<p><p>The snowboard helmet market is categorized into three main types: Full Shell, Half Shell, and Full Face helmets. Full Shell helmets provide comprehensive coverage, protecting the entire head while ensuring maximum safety. Half Shell helmets offer a lighter design, covering just the top and sides, making them suitable for casual riders looking for comfort and ventilation. Full Face helmets provide extensive protection, covering the entire head and face, which is ideal for aggressive riders or those engaging in high-speed downhill activities.</p></p>
