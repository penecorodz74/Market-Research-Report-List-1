<p><p>The DRAM probe card market comprises two primary types: MEMS probe cards and non-MEMS probe cards. MEMS probe cards utilize Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems technology, offering high precision and miniaturization, which enhances performance and reduces manufacturing costs. In contrast, non-MEMS probe cards are typically made of traditional materials and deliver reliable performance for various testing applications but may lack the advanced features of MEMS designs. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor testing, ensuring functionality and quality in DRAM production.</p></p>
