<p><ul><li>Air Pressure</li><li>Gravity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil lift drain market primarily features two types: air pressure systems and gravity systems. Air pressure systems utilize compressed air to force oil from reservoirs, facilitating efficient recovery and distribution. In contrast, gravity systems rely on the natural gravitational pull to move oil, often used in less complex operations where elevation changes can be leveraged. Both methods cater to different operational needs and environments, impacting efficiency, cost, and the overall effectiveness of oil extraction processes.</p></p>
