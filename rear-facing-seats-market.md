<p><p>The rear-facing seat market consists of three main types: rear-facing-only, convertible, and all-in-one seats. Rear-facing-only seats are designed exclusively for infants and provide enhanced safety for newborns. Convertible seats can be used both rear-facing and forward-facing, accommodating a child's growth. All-in-one seats offer the most versatility, functioning as rear-facing, forward-facing, and booster seats, making them a long-term investment for families. Each type prioritizes safety and comfort while catering to different stages of a child's development.</p></p>
