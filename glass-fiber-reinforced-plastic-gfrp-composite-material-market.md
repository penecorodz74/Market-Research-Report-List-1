<p><p>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastic (GFRP) composite material market is divided into two main types: thermosetting and thermoplastic plastic products. Thermosetting plastics are formed by curing processes that create irreversible chemical bonds, resulting in durable, heat-resistant materials, ideal for applications such as automotive and aerospace parts. In contrast, thermoplastic plastics can be reshaped with heat, offering versatility and ease of manufacturing. GFRP is widely used in construction, marine, and electronics industries due to its lightweight, strength, and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
