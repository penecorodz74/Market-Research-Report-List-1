<p><strong>The Oud Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distilled Based</li><li>Extracted Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oud oil market predominantly consists of two types: distilled-based and extracted-based. Distilled-based oud oil is produced through steam distillation of agarwood, resulting in a more refined and concentrated product with high purity. In contrast, extracted-based oud oil is obtained using solvents or other methods, which may yield a richer, more complex scent but often compromises purity. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and applications, influencing their pricing, market demand, and overall fragrance profiles.</p></p>
