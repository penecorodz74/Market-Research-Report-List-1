<p><p>The software-defined satellite market encompasses three primary types based on orbit: Medium Earth Orbit (MEO), Low Earth Orbit (LEO), and Geosynchronous Earth Orbit (GEO). MEO satellites provide a balance between coverage and latency, ideal for navigation and communication applications. LEO satellites offer lower latency and high-speed connectivity for diverse applications, making them suitable for broadband services. GEO satellites maintain a fixed position relative to Earth, supporting traditional communication and broadcasting but face higher latency. Each orbit type caters to specific industry needs.</p></p>
