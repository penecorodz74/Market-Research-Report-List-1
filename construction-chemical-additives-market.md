<p><p>The construction chemical additives market includes various types that enhance the performance and properties of construction materials. Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose (HPMC) is used as a thickening and water-retaining agent, improving the workability of mortars and plasters. Hydroxyethyl Methylcellulose (HEMC) offers similar benefits while providing better adhesion. Carboxymethyl Cellulose (CMC) functions as a thickener and stabilizer, ensuring uniformity in products like paints and coatings. These additives play crucial roles in enhancing durability, flexibility, and application efficiency in construction.</p></p>
