<p><p>The Airport Radar market encompasses various types essential for ensuring safe and efficient flight operations. Surveillance radar monitors air traffic and identifies aircraft locations, while weather radar assesses conditions for safer takeoffs and landings. Approach radar guides aircraft during their descent and landing phases, improving precision. Primary radar detects aircraft through reflected signals, and secondary radar interacts with aircraft transponders for enhanced identification and data accuracy. Together, these systems ensure comprehensive airspace management and operational safety.</p></p>
