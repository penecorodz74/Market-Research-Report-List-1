<p><p>The invasive brain monitoring device market comprises various technologies. Electroencephalography (EEG) devices record electrical activity, while Magnetoencephalography (MEG) devices measure magnetic fields generated by neural activity. Transcranial Doppler (TCD) devices assess blood flow in the brain, and Intracranial Pressure (ICP) monitors track pressure levels. Cerebral oximeters measure oxygen levels, while MRI and CT devices provide detailed brain imaging. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) visualizes metabolic processes, and Electromyography (EMG) devices assess muscle activity, all contributing to comprehensive brain health monitoring.</p></p>
