<p><p>The fish meal market consists of three main types: whole fish meal, semi-skimmed fish meal, and defatted fish meal. Whole fish meal is made from whole fish, retaining most nutrients and oils, making it highly nutritious. Semi-skimmed fish meal has some fat removed, balancing cost and nutrition for various animal feed applications. Defatted fish meal has most of the oil extracted, offering a higher protein concentration, suitable for livestock and aquaculture, focusing on affordability and efficiency in feed formulations.</p></p>
