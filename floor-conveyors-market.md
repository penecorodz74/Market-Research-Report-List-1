<p><p>Floor conveyors are essential in material handling, offering various types suited for different applications. Belt conveyors transport materials smoothly along a continuous loop. Slat conveyors use slats for handling heavier loads. Bucket elevators lift materials vertically using buckets attached to a chain. Telescopic conveyors extend and retract for flexible loading. Towland conveyors utilize a tow system for moving goods. Gravity conveyors rely on gravity for movement, while powered roller conveyors use powered rollers for efficient transport. Turnkey systems provide complete solutions for customized needs.</p></p>
