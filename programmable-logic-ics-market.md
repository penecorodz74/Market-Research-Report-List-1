<p><p>The programmable logic ICS market consists of three main types: Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), Distributed Control Systems (DCS), and Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems. PLCs automate discrete processes and are widely used in manufacturing. DCSs manage complex processes in industries such as oil and gas, ensuring centralized control and monitoring. SCADA systems facilitate real-time data acquisition and control over large geographical areas, often incorporating remote management for utilities and critical infrastructure, enhancing operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
