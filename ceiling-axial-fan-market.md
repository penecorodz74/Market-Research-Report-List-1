<p><p>The ceiling axial fan market primarily consists of two types: AC axial fans and DC axial fans. AC axial fans are powered by alternating current and are typically used for their efficiency and cost-effectiveness in larger settings. In contrast, DC axial fans utilize direct current and offer higher energy efficiency, quieter operation, and advanced speed control, making them suitable for residential and commercial environments where energy savings and performance are prioritized. Both types cater to diverse ventilation needs and preferences.</p></p>
