<p><p>Through-Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensors are specialized devices designed to measure temperature by detecting infrared radiation emitted from objects. These sensors consist of thermocouples configured in a thermopile arrangement, allowing them to convert thermal energy into an electrical signal. Their through-hole design makes them suitable for automated insertion into circuit boards, ensuring reliable thermal measurements in various applications such as HVAC systems, medical devices, and consumer electronics.</p><p>The Through-Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensor Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by an increasing demand for effective temperature measurement solutions across industrial and consumer sectors.
