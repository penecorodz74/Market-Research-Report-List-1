<p><p>The Contact Center Outsourcing Service market is primarily divided into two types: Partial Outsourcing and Complete Outsourcing. Partial Outsourcing involves delegating specific functions, such as customer support or technical assistance, while the core operations remain in-house. This allows businesses to maintain control over essential processes. In contrast, Complete Outsourcing entails transferring all contact center operations to a third-party provider, ensuring comprehensive management of customer interactions. This approach offers scalability and expertise but may reduce direct oversight of customer service quality.</p></p>
